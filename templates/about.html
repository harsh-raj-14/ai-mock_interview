About AI Mock Interview

An AI-powered technical interview simulation platform designed to help students and professionals prepare with real-world precision.

Conceptualized and developed by Gaddam Likhitha Raj.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between academic preparation and real-world technical interviews by leveraging advanced AI models. Our platform dynamically generates role-specific questions, evaluates responses intelligently, and provides actionable feedback to improve performance.

How It Works
  • AI generates dynamic, role-based interview questions
  • Responses are evaluated using intelligent scoring models
  • Real-time feedback improves conceptual clarity
  • Performance dashboard tracks growth over time
Why This Platform Stands Out
Unlike traditional mock interviews, this system adapts dynamically using AI. Each session is unique. The scoring engine evaluates depth, clarity, and technical relevance — simulating real interview conditions with intelligent feedback loops.
